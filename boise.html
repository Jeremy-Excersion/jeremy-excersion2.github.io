Welcome to Boise, Idaho's Capital City

Idaho's state capital, Boise, is known as "The City of Trees".

Boise is the most populated city in Idaho with over 235,000 residents. It was incorporated in 1864. Over the years Boise, Meridian, Eagle, and Kuna have expanded to where their borders touch. You hardly feel a difference as you travel into the next city. This has became known as the Boise Metropolitan Area.

Boise is located in the southwest region of Idaho, only 45 minutes away from Oregon.

The average income of Boise is slightly above average when compared to the rest of Idaho.

Cities in Order of Population

  1. Boise
  2. Meridian
  3. Nampa
  4. Caldwell
  5. Eagle
  6. Kuna
