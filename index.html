Welcome to the State of Idaho

Idaho is a state in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. Many people enjoy Idaho for the beautiful outdoor scenery that it has to offer. Idaho offers full four seasons each year with many activities in Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter.

We have thousands of miles of rivers for fishing, kayaking, whitewater rafting. Fun fact: Idaho has more miles of rivers than any other state in the United States.

For more information about things to do while you visit Idaho, check out VisitIdaho.org
