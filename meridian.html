Meridian, Idaho's Second Largest City

Meridian is the second largest city in Southwestern Idaho with over 117,000 residents. It was incorporated in 1909.

Meridian is located just West of Boise and the boundaries actually touch. It's difficult to tell where the line is that differentiates

whether you are in Meridian or Boise.

The suburban lifestyle of living in Meridian gives off a close knit feel. The average income is generally above average compared to the rest of Idaho.
