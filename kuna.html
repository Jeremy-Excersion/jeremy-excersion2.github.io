Kuna, Idaho

Living in Kuna, Idaho is a great place to live if you enjoy a small town feel. Kuna is located just South of Boise, Idaho. Kuna is a rural town with a population of less than 25,000 people. The average income is slightly above average compared to the rest of Idaho. Kuna is known for having houses on acreage. Many people enjoy the rural lifestyle that Kuna has to offer.

Kuna offers a wedding venue called The Castle Gardens where my wife and I got married on November 3rd, 2023. Below is a photo of the venue with the front of the castle.

Why Live in Kuna?

  1. Small town feel (< 30,000 people)
  2. Close to Boise (SW Idaho)
  3. Houses on Acreage
  4. Rural Town
